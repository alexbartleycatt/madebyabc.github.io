Intelligent Avatars for IDX Group

Lead Designer and Researcher

We helped one of Australasia's largest radiology providers deploy an intelligent avatar across multiple brands under the Integral Diagnostics umbrella. The avatar is designed to make booking an appointment effortless for patients, which can be done from any device, 24/7. Being integrated directly with administrative and clinical systems creates efficiency and saves staff time.

Referral Automation for Mercy Radiology

Project Lead and Designer

We helped Mercy Radiology develop a referral automation solution. Using a mix of OCR, layout detection, and process automation, this solution extracts key fields from dozens of varied referral documents and identifies required information. This allowed staff to spend their time on more important work, rather than reading and entering countless referrals each day.

Digital Transformation for NZ Police

Lead Designer and Facilitator

We facilitated a comprehensive design process with NZ Police to understand how they could improve service delivery to the public. This involved interviews, observation, and workshops, working closely with the organisation to identify and address key challenges in their digital transformation journey. Tech Education for SeniorNet NZ

Project Lead and Tutor

SeniorNet is an amazing organisation that exists to help anyone over the age of 65 make better use of digital technology. I was lucky enough to guide the creation of two courses focussed on; using Google Search to find information, and using Google Drive for personal creativity and admin. We did this to give senior learners the tools to develop their skills and confidence outside of the classroom.
